About Vistage Worldwide Inc.

✻

Vistage Worldwide is designedexclusively for high-integrity CEOs and executive leaders who are looking to drive better decisions and better results for their companies, families and communities. Our members gather in confidential peer groups, led by an accomplished business leader, to gather fresh perspectives and objective advice on their toughest challenges. Since 1957, Vistage's formula has withstood the test of time. Today, more than 22,000 executives across 20 countries rely on Vistage.

Companies who joined Vistage over the past five years grew 2.2x faster than average small and medium-sized U.S. businesses, according to a 2017 analysis.

No one understands the challenges and needs of CEOs and key executives better than Vistage. For more than 60 years, Vistage has been helping business leaders around the world refine their instincts, improve their judgment and expand their perspectives to make better decisions. Better decisions lead to better results, and the results speak for themselves.
